Compute Resource Platform

This site provides an overview of the compute resources available at our Chair of Computational Neuroscience. Use the navigation to inspect clusters and servers, review usage history, and manage user access.

What you can do here

  • Overview: High-level status of compute infrastructure.
  • Servers (Work in Progress): Per-server health, specs, and availability.
  • History (Work in Progress): Recent jobs, utilization, and events.
  • Users (Work in Progress): Get an overview of processes of a single user.
  • Docker (Work in Progress): Container images and runtime information.
